Frequently Asked Questions




What are Fey Forest, Swanson Sewers,
Mulaney Madlands and Gasteyer Garden?

Fey Forest: A forest that is the main home of the Yoshis in Yoshyrule.

Swanson Sewers: A sewer filled area where snakes are home to.

Gasteyer Garden: A garden area located in Fey Forest that is the Yoshis' happy place.
It is the only place where the Zombie scourage does not take place.

Mulaney Madlands: A hostile environment ruled by Dratchy Downerhausen.
This is where Princess Zora is locked up.

NOTE TO READER: Some items, enemies and treasure chests are absent in different areas.



Who is Dratchy Downerhausen?

Dratchy Downerhausen is the antagonist of Yoshyrule. He kidnaps
Princess Zora, as well as being the one who caused a Zombie scourage to occur.

Downerhausen can only be found in the castle area of
Mulaney Madlands, where Zora is locked up.

NOTE TO READER: Unlike the snakes or horses, he has 800 HP.



How many songs are in this game?

There are 8 songs overall, most from various media and games.
