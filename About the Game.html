What do you do in this game?

In this game, you have to save Princess Zora from an
evil monster, named Dratchy Downerhausen, putting the Yoshyrule tribe at risk.

Throughout the game, non-playable, different-colored Yoshis appear,
who are important on your adventure:

The purple Yoshi (real name, Yoehler Poehlerberg) talks about
what is happening in the game.

The blue Yoshi (real name, Yoshneese) is the shopkeeper of Yoshyrule.

The red Yoshi (real name, Yabe Yoscheinwald) is your tutorial guide and companion.

------------------------------------------------

To control Yoshi, use the WASD buttons or arrow keys.
To talk to the Yoshis/NPCS and/or interact with objects, you use the space button.
