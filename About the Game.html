What do you do in this game?

In this game, you have to save Princess Zora (a parody of Zelda) from an evil monster,
putting the Yoshyrule (a parody of Legend of Zelda's Hyrule) tribe at risk.

Throughout the game, non-playable, different-colored Yoshis appear:

The Purple Yoshi talks about what is happening in the game.

The blue Yoshi is the shopkeeper of Yoshyrule.

The red Yoshi is your tutorial guide.

To control Yoshi, use the WASD buttons or arrow keys.
To talk to the Yoshis/NPCS and/or interact with objects, you use the space button.
